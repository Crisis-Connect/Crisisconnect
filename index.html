About this tool: CrisisConnect uses AI to match your situation to known support organisations in Bangalore, to read and translate documents, and to screen uploaded images and messages. AI can make mistakes — availability, translations, and legitimacy checks may be wrong. Always confirm with the organisation. This tool does not replace emergency services. For life-threatening emergencies, call 108 immediately. The AI does not decide your eligibility for any service — the organisation decides when you contact them. Reports forwarded to authorities and alerts to nearby users are simulated in this demo build.
